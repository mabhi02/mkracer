Why MK?

“If you can leave two black stripes from the exit of one corner to the braking zone of the next, you have enough horsepower ─ Mark Donohue

In today's ever more virtual world, people are spending more time online playing video games. For this reason, we created MK Racer, a free and online car racer. While other games may feature traditional races, our game also features an ML driven autonomous driving system trained with real life car data.
